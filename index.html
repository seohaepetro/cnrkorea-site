About CNR Korea

CNR Korea Co., Ltd., founded on November 11, 1999, has been continuously engaged in the development of environmental technologies for over two decades.

We develop and deliver practical, scalable solutions for water treatment, renewable energy generation, and environmental restoration. Our work is driven by real-world challenges and validated through field deployment rather than laboratory-only concepts.

Our technologies are designed with a strong focus on low-energy operation, high efficiency, operational safety, durability, and ease of maintenance, enabling reliable performance across diverse environments, including remote and infrastructure-limited regions.

Vision

To deliver practical environmental and energy technologies that enable carbon neutrality, water security, and resilient infrastructure worldwide.

Mission

We design and deploy low-energy, high-efficiency systems that solve real-world environmental and energy challenges through scalable, field-proven solutions.

Why CNR Korea
